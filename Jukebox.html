JUKEBOX

Play from a selection of songs through a speaker. The song title will be displayed on the LCD screen. The LED's connected will light up and dance with the song.

Technologies

  • AVR Studio 6
  • ATmega1284
  • Speaker
  • 4x Push Button
  • 4x 330 Ohm Resistor
  • 4x 5mm LED
  • LCD Screen
  • 10k potentiometer
  • Power adapter
