About Me

Hi, thanks for checking my page out! I'm Wendy Li and I was born and raised in San Francisco, California. I studied Computer Science at the University of California, Riverside and got involved in several of my campus' STEM organizations.

With my involvement, I really wanted to dedicate my time to helping increase diversity in the field of STEM. I was the director of Rose Hack, my university's first ever women-centric hackathon. It was hugely successful with overwhelming support from over 40 corporate sponsors, students, and faculty. The event was partially hosted by UCR's engineering organization, Women In Computing of which I was the treasurer. With my dedication and passion for helping marginalized groups in the field of stem, I was awarded with a scholarship to Grace Hopper Celebration 2018 as a Grace Hopper Scholar.
